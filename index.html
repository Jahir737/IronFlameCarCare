Ultimate Paint Protection Film (PPF)

Experience unbeatable defence against scratches, chips, and UV damage.

Installing PPF (Paint Protection Film) on a car offers several benefits, including protection from scratches, stone chips, and UV damage, enhancing the car's appearance, and preserving resale value. It also makes the car easier to clean and maintain, and some PPFs have self-healing properties.

Protection Against Road Debris

PPF shields the paint from scratches, stone chips, and other road debris, preventing damage from impacts.

From UV Damage

It protects the paint from harmful UV rays, preventing fading and discoloration.

Against Chemicals and Corrosion

PPF helps protect the paint from chemicals used in road maintenance (salt and sand) and from acidic bird droppings or acid rain.

Enhanced Gloss and Shine

PPF can enhance the car's existing paint's gloss and shine, making it look newer for longer.

Self-Healing Properties

Some PPFs have self-healing properties that can repair minor scratches with heat.

Resists Stains and Contaminants

PPF resists stains and contaminants, making it easier to clean and maintain the car's appearance.

Increased Resale Value

Protecting the paint from damage helps preserve the car's original condition, potentially increasing its resale value.

Ease of Cleaning

PPF's hydrophobic nature makes it easier to clean and maintain, as water and dirt tend to bead up and roll off.

Durable and Long-Lasting

PPF is designed to last for several years, providing long-term protection for the car's paint.

Customizable

PPF can be custom-cut to fit any make and model of car, and can be applied to specific areas prone to damage.

Block Sun Damage

The film offers UV protection, blocking harmful rays that can fade paint.

Extends Paint's Lifespan

By protecting the paint from scratches, stains, and environmental damage, PPF significantly extends its lifespan.
